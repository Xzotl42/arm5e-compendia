### Plus / Minus signs

- Add / Remove a short term fatigue level
- Long term fatigue levels are not impacted.

### Plus / Minus signs with SHIFT key

- Add / Remove a long term fatigue level
- Short term fatigue levels are not impacted

### Rest button

- Remove all short term fatigue levels
- If no short term fatigue exists, remove **one** long term fatigue level.

### Rest button + SHIFT

- Remove all fatigue, long or short.
